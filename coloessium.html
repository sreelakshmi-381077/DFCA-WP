The colosseum,rome

The colosseum is an oval amphitheater in the center of the city,rome,italy,just east of the roman forum.it is the largest ancient amphitheater ever built ,and still the largest standing amphitheater in the world today,despite its age.construction began under the emperor vesapian(r.69-70 AD)in 72 and was completed in 80 AD under his sucessor and heir .Titus(r.79-81) further modification made during the reign of domitian(r.81-96).The three emperors that were patrons of the work are known as the flavin dynasty,and the amphitheatre were named the flavin amphitheatre.The coloesseum is built of travertine limestone,tuff and brick-faced concrete.
